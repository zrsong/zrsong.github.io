Welcome

Zirui Song is a PhD student at UWA Business School and an academic tutor at the School. His research lies at the intersection of accounting, finance, and economics, with a strong focus on how emerging technologies influence investor behaviour and the information environment. He has published in Accounting and Finance and Australian Journal of Management, and serves as an ad hoc reviewer for the International Journal of Accounting Information Systems.
